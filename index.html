What are stocks? and what are funds?

Stocks are shares of a company that can be bought and sold or us to invest in, the result depends on the earnings of the company we invested it. We will get some percentages of profit if the company got profits, but we will lose our money if the company loses money. Investing in stock is very risky, because you are going to invest in one company, there are chances that the company will go bankrupt, then you will all the money that you invested.

What are funds?

Funds are when many people put their money together, and experts someone who manage our money and use that money to buy many different investments and stocks and help growing everyone's money. Funds less chance of loss in money of the investment, because we are investing in many different kinds of companies and there are low chances of many different kinds of companies going bankrupt together.
